Online English pronunciation exercise: Difficult first names part 2

This is the second part of two online exercises on pronouncing first names in English. In this part, you will learn how to pronounce correctly 11 more common male and female first names which are difficult to say correctly in English.

If you haven't done the first part of this pronunciation exercise, I would recommend that you do that either before or after you have done this.

This exercise is for British English pronunciation.

To see all our online pronunciation exercises you can do, go to our 'English Pronunciation Exercises Menu'.

Exercise:

For each question in this online pronunciation exercise, first listen to the name by playing the question's audio player. Then say the name you hear. After this, spell the name in the text box and press the 'Check' button.

After you have spelt the word correctly, you should practise saying it. To do this, go to the web page of Google Translate or go to the Google Translate App on your mobile phone and practice saying the word by pressing on the microphone icon. Do it until the word you want to say is written on the screen in text.
