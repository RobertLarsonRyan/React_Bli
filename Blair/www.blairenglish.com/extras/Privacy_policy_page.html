Privacy Policy Page

Blairenglish.com is based in Madrid, Spain and may be contacted at contact@blairenglish.com. Like most websites, Blair English receives or actively collects personal data that may identify an individual visitor (you/your). This notice outlines the personal data we collect, how we use it and your rights concerning that data.

What personal data we collect

The personal data we routinely (attempt to) collect from you includes:

  • your IP address
  • your web browser details
  • details on how you use the site

In addition, we collect personal data that you knowingly and willingly provide, including:

  • When contacting us by email your name and email address
  • When ordering products from us via PayPal, your name and email, and your address and billing address if you so choose

In addition, we use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use the Site. Google Analytics stores information such as:

  • the pages a user visits on Blairenglish.com
  • how long a user spends on each Blairenglish.com page
  • how a user arrives at Blairenglish.com
  • what a user clicks on while visiting Blairenglish.com

The legal basis for collecting this personal data is to perform a task in your interest or for the maintenance and improvement of Blairenglish.com and its services.

We may share the personal data we collect with reputable supplier organisations (for example PayPal). We will never sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We will share your personal data if we are required to do so by law – for example, by court order, or to prevent fraud or other crime. We may transfer information about users if we are acquired by or merged with another company.

You consent to our cookies if you continue to use our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information that are automatically downloaded to your device when you visit a website. Cookies are useful because they allow a website to recognise your device and preferences, and can be used to improve your experience online.

What cookies do we use?

Functional

These cookies are used to ensure you can correctly navigate and use blairenglish.com, play videos via YouTube and make purchases via PayPal.

Performance

These cookies are used to analyse trends, administer the website, track visitor movements and gather demographic information for aggregate use and to help us improve blairenglish.com. These cookies are not personally identifiable.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser to accept or reject some or all cookies. For instructions on managing cookies, please see your browser’s Help section or visit AboutCookies.org. Be aware that some parts of blairenglish.com may not function after you change your cookie settings.

Advertising

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on blairenglish.com.
  • Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to blairenglish.com users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
