English vocabulary class worksheets/lesson plans

Downloadable exercise class worksheets

It's very often difficult to find good exercise worksheets for teaching students English vocabulary on specific subjects/topics. You can spend hours looking through books or the web and still not find something which you feel happy about using in class. I normally ended up spending hours writing my own exercises. Not an ideal situation for busy teachers.

To help you make better use of your time, I have created a number of English vocabulary exercise worksheets and lesson plans. Each has been designed to enable the students to work out for themselves the meaning of useful and important vocabulary on a specific topic. Allowing you to have one less thing to worry about in your busy day.

To try one for FREE and see for yourself, download the below exercise class worksheet with lesson plan on 'Food Tastes & Textures'.

Food Tastes & Textures Class Worksheet with Lesson Plan: FREE

This exercise teaches the students the English adjectives used for describing food tastes and textures.
