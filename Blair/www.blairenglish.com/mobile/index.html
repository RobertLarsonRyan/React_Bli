It can be difficult to improve your English. To help you do it, I made Blair English. A website with over 200 different vocabulary and pronunciation online and interactive exercises/tests, articles and guides.

All the exercises (on business English, social, email writing, job interviews, pronunciation etc...) on Blair English have been specially designed so you'll easily learn the right vocabulary, understand it, remember it and be able say or write it!
