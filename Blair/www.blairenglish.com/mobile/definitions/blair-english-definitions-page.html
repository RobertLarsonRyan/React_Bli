Business Vocabulary Definitions

What is Blair English Definitions?

Not sure what the meaning of a business term or phrase means. You can look up a definition online. Unfortunatelty, most of the definitions you'll find online are either copied out of a dictionary or written for people who have a PHD in the topic. You'll probably end up being more confused about the word's/term's meaning after you've read the definition than you were before. But it doesn't have to be like this.

Welcome to Blair English Definitions. A collections of definitions of business, work and financial vocabulary and terms all written by myself to be easily understandable and in plain simple English.

To find a definition, type the word or phrase you want in the below box and then press 'search'.
