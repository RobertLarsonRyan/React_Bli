English Pronunciation Exercises & Articles

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do or the articles that you can read to improve your English pronunciation on Blair English.

For each exercise/article there is a description of what it is about. To do or look at one, simply click on the title of the exercise or article.


  • Online English Pronunciation Exercise/Test
    An online English pronunciation exercise to both learn and say/pronounce commonly mispronounced words in English.

  • Countries, Regions & Cities
    An online English pronunciation exercise on how to correctly say commonly mispronounced countries, regions & cities in English.

  • Difficult First Names Part 1
    The first of two online English pronunciation exercises on how to correctly say commonly mispronounced people's first names in English.

  • Difficult First Names Part 2
    The second of two online English pronunciation exercises on how to correctly say commonly mispronounced people's first names in English.

  • How to Speak Better English: Pronunciation
    Article on how to improve your English pronunciation quickly, easily and for free.
