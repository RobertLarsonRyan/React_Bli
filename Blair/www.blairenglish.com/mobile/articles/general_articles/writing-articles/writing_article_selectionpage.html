Improve your Writing Article Menu

Below you will find listed all of the different articles that you can read to improve your writing. These articles give advice and help for writing different types of things (e.g. emails, letters etc...) and how to write better in general (e.g. punctuation).

For each article there is a description of what it is about below the title. To read or look at an article, simply click on the title of the article.


  • How to Write a Business Email/Letter in English
    This article gives 10 tips to write a good email & letter in English for both business and non-business use.

  • How to Write an Email to a Friend
    This article explains what you need to know to do to write good emails to friends both easily and quickly.

  • Improve your English Punctuation: How to Write Sentences
    This article explains a simple method to use commas and periods/full stops correctly in your writing.

  • How to Write a Good Sales Follow-Up Email
    Advice on how to write very good sales follow-up emails to both business and non-business customers.

  • Excuses to not Attend a Meeting
    Learn not only what type of excuses you can use in an email to not go to a meeting, but when (and when not) to use them.

  • How to Write Feedback in Emails
    This article explains how to give people feedback by email which doesn't sound like criticism.
