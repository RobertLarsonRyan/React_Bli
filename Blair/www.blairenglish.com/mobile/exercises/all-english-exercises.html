English Exercises List

Below is a list of all the online English vocabulary and grammar exercises that you can do to improve your English on Blair English.

All the exercises are grouped by catergory (e.g. Food and Drink, Emails etc...). In addition to learning the vocabulary, each exercise (except the exercises on emails and writing) has a pronunciation test for the vocabulary which will help you say the words and phrases perfectly in English.

These exercises are designed for both adults and teenagers to do.

To do an exercise, click/press on the exercise name.
