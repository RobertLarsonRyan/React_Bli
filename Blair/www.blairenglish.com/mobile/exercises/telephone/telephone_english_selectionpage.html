Telephone English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your use of English vocabulary when speaking on the telephone on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Essential Telephone English Vocabulary Part 1
    Level: Lower-Intermediate
    In this first part of two exercises you will learn the meaning of essential English vocabulary used when both talking on the telephone and talking about telephone calls.

  • Essential Telephone English Vocabulary Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    In this second part of two exercises you will learn the meaning of English phrasal verbs and expressions that are commonly used when both talking on the telephone and talking about telephone calls.

  • English Telephone Phrases: Calling Somebody Directly
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn what to say and what people will say to you when making telephone calls to somebody directly in English.

  • English Telephone Phrases: Asking to Speak to Somebody
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn what to say and what people will say to you when making telephone calls where you have to ask to speak to somebody in English.

  • English Telephone Phrases: Calling a Company
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn what to say and what people will say to you when making telephone calls in English to a company or organisation when you don't have a name of somebody to speak to

  • English Telephone Phrases for when the Person You Call Isn't there or Is Busy Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn phrases and what will be said in a telephone call in English when a person you are calling for is not there in this first of two online vocabulary exercises on the topic.

  • English Telephone Phrases for when the Person You Call Isn't there or Is Busy Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn more phrases (like how to leave a message) and what will be said in a telephone call in English when a person you are calling for is not there in this second of two online vocabulary exercises on English telephone phrases.
