Legal English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your knowledge and use of legal vocabulary on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Types of Crimes Against People Part 1
    Level: Advanced
    In this first of two online exercises you will learn the names and descriptions of crimes which are either violent or against people.

  • Types of Crimes Against People Part 2
    Level: Advanced
    Learn more of the names and descriptions in this second of two online exercises on crimes which are either violent or against people.

  • Types of Crimes Against Property or Theft
    Level: Advanced
    Learn the names and descriptions of crimes which are against property or involve theft in this online exercise.

  • Types of Financial Crime
    Level: Advanced
    Learn the names and descriptions of crimes which are financial in this online exercise.

  • Police Investigations and Actions Part 1: The Beginning of an Investigation
    Level: Advanced
    Learn the names of the actions which occur and what the police do at the beginning of an investigation into a crime in this of first of three online exercises on police investigations.
