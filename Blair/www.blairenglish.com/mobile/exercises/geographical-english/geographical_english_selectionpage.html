Geographical English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your use of English vocabulary connected to Geography and the natural world on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • English Geography Vocabulary: Water Landform Names
    Level: Intermediate
    In this online exercise you will learn the English names of geographical features connected to water in the countryside (in the mountains etc...).

  • English Geography Vocabulary: Coastal Landforms & The Sea
    Level: Intermediate
    In this online exercise you will learn the English names of geographical features connected to the sea and oceans in coastal areas.

  • English Geography Vocabulary: Mountain Landforms
    Level: Intermediate
    In this online exercise you will learn the English names of different geographical features that are found in mountainous regions.

  • English Vocabulary for Walking in The Countryside Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    In this first of two online exercises you will learn the names in English of places and things which you will see and use when walking in the countryside.

  • English Vocabulary for Walking in The Countryside Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    In this second of two online exercises you will learn the names in English of more places and things which you will see and use when walking in the countryside.
