50 Business English Phrasal Verbs

Phrasal verbs are commonly used in business situations where English is being used. So it is important that you can understand them when you hear them being used.

To help you do this, below you'll find a list of 50 phrasal verbs which are frequently used in business and work situations in English.

You will know some of these already, but there will be some that you don't know or are unsure about. To help you quickly learn the meaning of these phrasals verbs, click on the phrasal verb and this will take you to a YouTube video where you will learn its meaning and use.

To help you remember the meaning and use of the phrasal verb, after you have watched the video for the phrasal verb, create a sentence in your own words using it.

To learn the best way to remember new vocabulary in English (including phrasal verbs), read our article called 'A method for remembering vocabulary in English'.
