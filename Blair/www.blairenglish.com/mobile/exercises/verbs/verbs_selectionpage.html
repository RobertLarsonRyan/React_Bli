English Verb Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to improve your use and knowledge of English verbs and phrasal verbs on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • How to Use Past English Verbs
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn how and when to use four different verbs in English (past simple, past continuous, past perfect and present simple) to talk about the past.

  • English Verbs: How to Use 'To Do' and 'To Make'
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces how to use the verbs and when to use them.

  • How to Use the Present Perfect Verb Tense Correctly in English
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn five different uses of the present perfect verb tense in English and how to use them correctly.

  • English Phrasal Verb Videos
    Level: Intermediate
    Online videos to help you learn and remember the different meanings of some commonly used English phrasal verbs.

  • Phrasal Verb Daily Quizzes
    Level: Intermediate
    Quizzes for the phrasal verbs in the 'Phrasal Verb Daily' videos on YouTube.

  • 50 Business English Phrasal Verbs
    Level: Intermediate
    A list of 50 phrasal verbs that are commonly used in business situations in English.

  • English Verbs and Prepositions Part 1
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Part one of an exercise that focuses on the verbs and their prepositions that learners often make mistakes with.

  • English Verbs and Prepositions Part 2
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Part two of an exercise that focuses on the verbs and their prepositions that learners often make mistakes with.

  • How to Use English Phrasal Verbs Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    The first part of four exercises on how to correctly use phrasal verbs in English.

  • How to Use English Phrasal Verbs Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    The second part of four exercises on how to correctly use phrasal verbs in English.

  • How to Use English Phrasal Verbs Part 3
    Level: Intermediate
    The third part of four exercises on how to correctly use phrasal verbs in English.

  • How to Use English Phrasal Verbs Part 4
    Level: Intermediate
    The last part of four exercises on how to correctly use phrasal verbs in English.

  • Essential English Phrasal Verbs Used in Business Meetings
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces the essential phrasal verbs that are used when participating in business meetings.

  • English Phrasal Verbs for Explaining Problems Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    First part of two exercises on phrasal verbs that are used when explaining and trying to resolve problems in business meetings/projects and other situations.

  • English Phrasal Verbs for Explaining Problems Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Second part of two exercises on phrasal verbs that are used when explaining and trying to resolve problems in business meetings/projects and other situations.

  • How to Use the Verb 'To Get' in English Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    This first of two exercises on the verb 'to get' focuses on the different meanings of the verb in English and when and how it should be used.

  • How to Use the Verb 'To Get' in English Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    This second of two exercises on the verb 'to get' looks at verbs in English which some times can be replaced by 'to get', but other times can't.
