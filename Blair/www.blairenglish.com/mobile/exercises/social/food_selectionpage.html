Food and Drink English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your use of English vocabulary for food, drink and restaurants on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Essential English Vocabulary for Restaurants
    Level: Lower-Intermediate
    Essential words and phrases used when eating and ordering in a restaurant. The focus isn't on food vocabulary.

  • English Vocabulary for Food Cooking Methods
    Level: Intermediate
    Vocabulary for describing how food is cooked. Very useful for understanding a restaurant menu.

  • English Food Tastes & Textures Vocabulary Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    In this first of two online exercises learn some English vocabulary to describe the taste and texture of food.

  • English Food Tastes & Textures Vocabulary Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    In this second of two online exercises learn more English vocabulary to describe the taste and texture of food.

  • English Food Vocabulary: Types of Meat
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn and remember the names in English for the different types meat you can eat.

  • The English Names of the Things We Eat & Drink with Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names in English of the things that you use to eat with and from in this first of three exercises.

  • The English Names of the Things We Eat & Drink with Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names in English of the things that you use to drink with and from in this second of three exercises.

  • The English Names of the Things We Eat & Drink with Part 3
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names in English of the things not specifically used to eat or drink with or from, but which you find used when eating food in a restaurant or at home in this third of three exercises.

  • Vocabulary for Complaining/Problems in a Restaurant Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    This first of two online exercises looks at English vocabulary and phrases used in restuarants when complaining and explaining problems.

  • Vocabulary for Complaining/Problems in a Restaurant Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    This second of two online exercises continues to look at English vocabulary and phrases used in restuarants when complaining and explaining problems.

  • Essential Wine Vocabulary & Terms
    Level: Intermediate
    This exercise introduces the essential vocabulary for talking about, describing and ordering wine in English.

  • Wine Tasting Vocabulary & Words
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    This exercise looks at the vocabulary for describing the different tastes, smells and characteristics of wine.

  • Essential English Vocabulary for Drinking Alcohol
    Level: Intermediate
    This exercise looks at the vocabulary connected to drinking alcohol, drinking spirits/hard liquor and ordering in a pub.

  • English Vocabulary for Beer and Drinking
    Level: Intermediate
    This exercise looks at the English vocabulary for beer. The different types and ways to drink it.

  • English for Restaurant Waiters: When Customers Arrive
    Level: Intermediate
    English phrases and vocabulary for people who work in restaurants to use with customers when they arrive and when taking drink orders.

  • English for Restaurant Waiters: Taking Customers' Orders and Serving Food
    Level: Intermediate
    English phrases and vocabulary for people who work in restaurants to use for taking customer food orders and serving dishes to customers.

  • English for Restaurant waiters: Giving the Bill/Check and Saying Goodbye
    Level: Intermediate
    English phrases and vocabulary for people who work in restaurants to use after serving customers their food to when the customers leave the restaurant.
