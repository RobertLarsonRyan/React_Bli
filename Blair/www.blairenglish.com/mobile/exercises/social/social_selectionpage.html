Social English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your use of social vocabulary (e.g. shopping, entertainment, sports etc...) in English on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Essential English Vocabulary for Shopping
    Level: Lower-Intermediate
    Learn 13 English words and phrases that you will hear or use when shopping in English-speaking countries.

  • Vocabulary for Describing Films/Movies Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn in this first of two online exercises the English vocabulary you need to know to have a good and a fluid conversation with somebody about movies/films in English.

  • Vocabulary for Describing Films/Movies Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn in this second of two online exercises more English vocabulary to talk about movies/films.

  • Movie Genres Type/Name Vocabulary Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names in English of the different types of movies/films that people commonly watch.

  • How to Introduce Yourself in English Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn both ways and phrases which you can use (or you will hear used) to introduce yourself to someone in English in business and formal situations.

  • How to Introduce Yourself in English Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn more ways and phrases which you can use (or you will hear used) to introduce yourself to someone in English in business and formal situations.

  • How to Introduce People in English
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn different ways and phrases which you can use when you are introducing people to other people in English in business and formal situations.

  • How to Say Goodbye to People in English
    Level: Pre-Intermediate
    Learn different ways and phrases which you can use when saying goodbye to people in business and formal situations.

  • Vocabulary for Describing Paintings Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names for describing the different physical features of and in a painting in this first of two online exercises.

  • Vocabulary for Describing Paintings Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the vocabulary for describing the different types and styles of paintings in this second of two online exercises.

  • English Family Vocabulary: Family Member Names Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names in English for the members of a family which many learners of English have forgetten.

  • English Family Vocabulary: Family Member Names Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn some more advanced names in English for the different members of a family which most learners of English don't know.

  • English Vocabulary for Talking about Colleagues/Friends
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn phrases and vocabulary that you can use when having a conversation in English with somebody and you are talking about whats's happening to people you both know.

  • English Vocabulary for Describing an Illness & Symptoms
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn 16 commonly used words and phrases in English that people use when talking about illness and not feeling physically well.

  • Sports Injuries English Vocabulary
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the names in English of different injuries which people often suffer when doing sport.

  • English Vocabulary for Describing Clothes
    Level: Lower-Intermediate
    Learn the names in English for the different parts and styles (e.g. designs) of clothes.

  • Shopping for Clothes Vocabulary Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn English words and phrases that you can use (or hear used) when shopping, buying and asking about clothes in a clothes shop/store.

  • Shopping for Clothes Vocabulary Part 2
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn more English words and phrases that you can use (or hear used) when shopping, buying and asking about clothes in a clothes shop/store.

  • English Vocabulary for Television and TV Programs/Shows
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the English vocabulary used to talk about television and fictional TV shows/programs.

  • How to Describe a TV Program/Show Vocabulary
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Learn the English words for describing the different parts and roles in fictional TV shows/programs.

  • Political English Vocabulary: Elections Part 1
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Learn the English vocabulary used to both talk about and describe what happens in political elections.

  • Political English Vocabulary: Elections Part 2
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Learn more English vocabulary used to both talk about and describe what happens in political elections.

  • English Vocabulary for Football Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the English vocabulary that is used for describing what football/soccer is and what happens in a match/game.

  • English Vocabulary for Football Part 2
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Learn the English vocabulary for describing the different things that football players do with the ball when playing.

  • World Cup English Football Vocabulary
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn all the English vocabulary you need for talking about the football World Cup and other football tournaments.

  • Street Directions in English Vocabulary
    Level: Lower-Intermediate
    Learn 12 English phrases and words to make it easy to both give and understand directions to places in English.
