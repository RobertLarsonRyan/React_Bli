Business Meeting Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your use of vocabulary in business meetings on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Essential Business English Meeting Vocabulary
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces fundamental meeting vocabulary.

  • How to Agree & Disagree in Business Situations
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces phrases to agree and disagree with people.

  • How to Apologize for Being Late
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces phrases and vocabulary to apologise for being late and to explain.

  • How to Confirm and Clarify Information
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces phrases to confirm and clarify information said in a meeting.

  • Business Decision Making Vocabulary/Phrases
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces phrases for the decision making process.

  • Can't Attend a Meeting Email
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces vocabulary to politely say you can't attend an event (e.g. Meeting, Presentation etc...).

  • How to Write a Business Email of Invitation
    Level: Intermediate
    This exercise looks at the English vocabulary and phrases used for writing for both formal and less formal business emails of invitation.

  • How to Write an Email to Make Somebody Attend a Meeting
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    This exercise explains how to persuade somebody by email to attend a meeting they said they couldn't go to.

  • How to Finish a Meeting Vocabulary
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces phrases that are used to summarize, close and organise the next meeting.

  • How to Interrupt & How to Respond
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces phrases for interrupting somebody and also ways of dealing with interruptions.

  • Probability Phrases Used in Business English
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces phrases that are used to express different levels of probability.

  • Essential English Phrasal Verbs Used in Business Meetings
    Level: Intermediate
    Introduces the essential phrasal verbs that are used when participating in business meetings.

  • English Phrasal Verbs for Explaining Problems Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Phrasal verbs that are used when explaining and trying to resolve problems in business meetings.
