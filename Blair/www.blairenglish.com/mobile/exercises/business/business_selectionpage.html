General Business English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your general business English vocabulary on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Economics Vocabulary: Describing how the Economy Is Performing
    Learn the English vocabulary (and its meaning) which is used to describe how well an economy is doing/performing.
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Business English Expressions Part 1
    Learn the meaning of 5 commonly used expressions that are used in business in English.
    Level: Intermediate

  • Business English Expressions Part 2
    Learn the meaning of 5 more commonly used expressions that are used in business in English.
    Level: Intermediate

  • English Vocabulary for Parts of an Office Part 1
    Learn the names in English names of 9 things and places that are normally found in an office or a place of work.
    Level: Lower-Intermediate

  • English Vocabulary for Parts of an Office Part 2
    Learn the names in English names of 8 more things and places that are normally found in an office or a place of work.
    Level: Lower-Intermediate

  • Corporate/Business Job Hierarchy Vocabulary
    Learn the names in English of the different types of job positions (and what their responsibilities are) that you will find in any company or organisation.
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Business English Time Phrases & Vocabulary
    Learn the vocabulary used in English to talk about when things happen or are done.
    Level: Intermediate

  • Vocabulary for Describing Business Relationships
    Learn the vocabulary used in English (and what it means) to describe the different types of relationships companies have when they work together.
    Level: Intermediate

  • Describing Work & Business Processes Part 1
    Learn the names in English of 11 common processes and tasks that most people do in their job at work. This vocabulary is essential for describing to people what you normally do in your job.
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Describing Work & Business Processes Part 2
    Learn the names in English of 11 more common processes and tasks that most people do in their job at work. This vocabulary is essential for describing to people what you normally do in your job.
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Business English Time Phrases for Requesting & Responding
    Learn 11 English phrases that you will commonly hear and can use to say when things will or won't be done.
    Level: Intermediate

  • How to Ask for Help
    Learn 10 English phrases you can use when asking somebody to do something for you and when trying to convince them to say 'yes' to what you want them to do.
    Level: Intermediate

  • How to Say 'NO' to a Request for Help
    Learn what to say in English when you don't want to do something which somebody has asked you to do.
    Level: Intermediate

  • Phrases for Criticising People
    Learn 15 phrases in English to politely criticise people at work for making mistakes and doing things wrong.
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Phrases for Reacting to Criticism
    Learn what to say in English when you are being criticised for making mistakes and doing things wrong.
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Absence from Work
    Learn the English vocabulary used when people don't go to work (e.g. becuase they are ill) and the consequences it can cause.
    Level: Intermediate

  • Losing a Job Vocabulary
    Learn the English vocabulary used when people lose their job (why and what happens as a consequence).
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • Leaving a Job, Resigning or Retiring Vocabulary
    Learn the English vocabulary used when people choose to leave their job (why and what happens as a consequence).
    Level: Upper-Intermediate

  • 10 English Work Phrasal Verbs
    Learn and remember 10 commonly used English phrasal verbs that are used in work situations.
    Level: Intermediate
