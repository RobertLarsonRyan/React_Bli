Aviation English Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your aviation English vocabulary on Blair English.

These exercises have been designed to help pilots and air traffic controllers to learn and improve their aviation English vocabulary.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Aviation English Vocabulary: Parts of an Airport
    Level: Lower-Intermediate
    Learn the English names of the different parts of an airport which pilots and controllers need to know.

  • Aviation English: Parts of a Plane Vocabulary Part 1
    Level: Intermediate
    Learn the English names of the main parts of an aircraft in this first of two online exercises on parts of a plane vocabulary.

  • Aviation English: Parts of a Plane Vocabulary Part 2
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Learn the English names of the different parts of an aircraft which make it move in this second of two online exercises on parts of a plane vocabulary.

  • Aviation English: Flight Instrument Panel Vocabulary
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Learn the names in English and uses of the main instruments used to fly a plane on a flight instrument panel on an aircraft.

  • Aviation English Vocabulary: Instructions when Landing
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Looks at and explains the English vocabulary & instructions used between pilots and controllers when landing a plane at airports.

  • Airport Pilot Instruction Vocabulary Part 1: Leaving the Terminal Gate
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    This first of four exercises shows and explains the English vocabulary used between pilots and controllers when a plane leaves a gate at an airport terminal.

  • Airport Pilot Instruction Vocabulary Part 2: Moving around an Airport
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    This second of four exercises shows and explains the English vocabulary used between pilots and controllers when moving around the ground at an airport.

  • Airport Pilot Instruction Vocabulary Part 3: Moving around an Airport Continued
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    This third of four exercises shows and explains more English vocabulary used between pilots and controllers when moving around the ground at an airport.

  • Airport Pilot Instruction Vocabulary Part 4: Taking Off from a Runway
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    This last of four exercises shows and explains the English vocabulary used between pilots and controllers before, during and just after taking off from an airport.
