Business Negotiations Vocabulary Exercises

Below you will find listed all the different exercises that you can do to learn or improve your use of vocabulary and strategies in business negotiations on Blair English.

For each exercise there is a description of what it is about and what level of English you need to do it (from 'lower-intermediate' to 'advanced').

To do or look at an exercise, simply click on the box or the title of the exercise.


  • Essential Negotiation Vocabulary Part 1
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces essential negotiation vocabulary and the names of basic negotiation strategies.

  • Essential Negotiation Vocabulary Part 2
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces negotiation vocabulary to describe the progression and events that happen in negotiations.

  • Negotiation Phrases: Making a Deal
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces negotiation phrases to improve your part of a deal and to suggest a compromise.

  • Delaying Business Negotiations Vocabulary & Phrases
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces negotiation phrases used to delay or slow down a negotiation.

  • Negotiations Phrases: Convincing people to buy
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces negotiation phrases that can be used to convince somebody that both your product/service and offer are the best.

  • Negotiations Phrases: Dealing with Unreasonable Demands
    Level: Upper-Intermediate
    Introduces phrases that can be used when the other side/party is being difficult/unreasonable in a negotiation.
