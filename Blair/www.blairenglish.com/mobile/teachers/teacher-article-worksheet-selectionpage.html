Blair English Teacher Article & Worksheet Menu

Below you will find listed all the different articles and material (e.g. worksheets etc...) which is available on Blair English to help teachers who teach English as a second language.

For each item listed there is a short description which explains what it is and/or what it does. To look at one, simply click on the title.

To see all of our online vocabulary exercises for students, go to the 'blair english exercise menu'.


  • How to Teach English Vocabulary to Adult Students
    Why both the choice of vocabulary taught in adult English classes and how it is taught are important for making productive and happy classes.

    Six recommended podcasts in English that teachers can use with their students to improve their listening and English pronunciation.

  • How to Get Your Students to Read More in English
    Learn why many students don't regularly read in English outside of class and what you can do to make sure that they do.

    Learn the reasons why learners often don't remember the meaning of new words and phrases in English and what they need to do to make sure that they do.

  • A Method for Remembering Vocabulary in English
    A simple method/way of learning English vocabulary that can be taught to students to make sure they remember the meaning of new words and phrases in English.

  • How to Teach Restaurant and Food English Vocabulary
    Advice on both what topics/subjects and how vocabulary for restaurants, food and drink should be taught to English students.

  • How to Teach Financial English Vocabulary to Students
    Advice on both what topics/subjects and how financial English vocabulary should be taught to students studying English.

  • How to Teach Business Meeting English Vocabulary
    Advice on both what topics/subjects and how business meeting vocabulary should be taught to students studying English.

    A list of the downloadable exercise class worksheets/lesson plans for teaching business and social English to students.
