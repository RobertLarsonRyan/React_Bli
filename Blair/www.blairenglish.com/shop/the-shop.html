Online Courses


Below are the online paid courses that I recommend you use to improve your English. I have tried them all myself and none of them are expensive.


English Class 101

English Class 101 is an online course where you'll learn English in an innovative different way. This online course helps you to improve your English by using audio (podcasts) and video lessons to teach and explain (in English) vocabulary, phrases and grammar. These lessons are reinforced with interactive vocabulary & pronunciation games and exercises.

If you don't like the traditional methods of learning English, you should try using their online course.

Level: All levels (from beginner to advanced).

Cost: $25 (US) for a one month subscription.

To see if you like their course, they offer a free 7 day trail.
